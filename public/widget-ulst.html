Oldham to learn Scholes decision on Thursday

Oldham will discover on Thursday if they can appoint Paul Scholes as manager, sources have told ESPN FC.

Oldham, a founder member of the Premier League in 1992, have been without a full-time manager since the sacking of Frankie Bunn on Dec. 27. Pete Wild, the club's academy coach, has since taken charge on a caretaker basis.
